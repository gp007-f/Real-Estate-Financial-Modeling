# Real-Estate-Financial-Modeling
a monthly pro forma for a 60-unit townhome project modeling land acquisition, construction draws, sales absorption, and debt service; outputs include levered IRR, peak equity, ROE, and a two-variable sensitivity table on sale price vs. development costs.

### Glossary
1) LTV (Loan to Value) -- The amt of debt financing a lender will provide as a percent of the market value 
2) LTC (Loan to Cost) -- The amount of debt financing a lender will provide as a percent of the cost of a development
3) NOI (Net Operating Income) -- Gross rental revenue less operating expenses (property taxes, insurance, repairs & maintenance, capital expenditures)
4) Cap Rate (Capitalization Rate) -- NOI (net operating income) divided by the market value of property expressed as a percent
GP -- unlimited liability
LP -- liability is limited to the amount of capital they invested in the project
FSR(Floor Space Ratio) -- The ratio of land to building area used to measure or limit the density of a parcel of land
GBA (Gross Building Area) -- The sum of all building spaces typically measured from external wall to external wall
